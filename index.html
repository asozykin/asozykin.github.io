Risk-management presentation example

This is an example of what a presentation for a bank's Asset-Liability committee might look like. It describes a situation of an elevated Interest Rate Risk and a way out of it proposed by an ALM Risk Management division.
Volumes of the bank's portfolios are made-up, but the corresponding durations (as well as the bank's LDR) are set to be realistic.
Risk-measures have been aprroximated in a spreadsheet.
